What We Do

Heating & Cooling Services in Novi MI

From emergency furnace repairs to full AC installations — we handle it all for homes and businesses across Novi and the Metro Detroit area.

Furnace Repair

Furnace stopped working on the coldest night of the year? We're specialists in furnace repair and offer 24-hour emergency service. Our technicians diagnose the problem fast and get your heat back on as quickly and economically as possible.

Furnace Installation

When repair isn't the right call, we'll sit down with you and go over the best options for a new furnace based on your home and budget. We carry top name-brand furnaces with extended warranties — and financing is available so you don't have to pay it all upfront.

AC Repair

Air conditioning stopped working on the hottest day of the year? We can typically get someone to your home or business the same day or next day. Many AC repairs are fast and inexpensive — we'll always review the options with you before any work is done.

AC Installation

When an AC unit is too old to repair economically, we'll analyze the situation and go over your best options. We'll recommend the right size unit for your home and budget. Financing is available — you don't have to come up with the full cost upfront.

AC Maintenance

The hot Michigan summers put serious strain on your AC unit. We recommend an annual tune-up to inspect filters, coils, freon levels, condensate drains, and coil fins — keeping your system running efficiently and avoiding costly breakdowns.

24/7 Emergency Service

Heating and cooling emergencies don't wait for business hours. Most of the time we offer 24-hour emergency service so that when something goes wrong in the middle of the night, we're here for you. Call us any time.

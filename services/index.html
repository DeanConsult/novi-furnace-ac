Annual Tune-Up

AC Maintenance

The hot Michigan summers put serious strain on your air conditioning unit. Consistent maintenance is important to keep your system running efficiently and avoid costly breakdowns. We recommend having your AC inspected and cleaned at least once per year.

Our technicians will inspect the filter, coils, freon, condensate drains, coil fins, and numerous other components to make sure everything is in working order. A small investment in maintenance can save you from a much more expensive repair down the road.

Our Story

Novi MI's Local HVAC Experts

Novi Furnace and Air Conditioning has been serving the Novi MI community and the broader Metro Detroit area for over 20 years. We started as a small local HVAC company with a simple goal: provide great service at a fair price and treat every customer the way we'd want to be treated.

That approach has worked. Most of our business today comes from repeat customers and referrals — people who called us years ago and keep coming back. We're proud of the reputation we've built in the Novi Michigan area and work hard every day to keep it.

We service both residential and commercial customers and can handle almost any heating and cooling issue. All of our technicians have years of experience and constantly undergo updated training so we stay current on new HVAC technology and the most efficient methods of repair and installation.
